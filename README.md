# Privacy Policy: My Tamagotchi
Effective date: July 1, 2026

My Tamagotchi is a virtual pet game. This policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices you have. The short version: we do not collect, store, or sell your personal information. Your pet lives on your device, and your game data never leaves it. The only third-party data processing happens through Google AdMob, which shows ads in a child-safe, non-personalized configuration.

1. Information we collect
We collect none. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no chat, no social features, and no servers of our own. Everything the game creates — your pet's name, stats, coins, gems, album, and settings — is stored only in the app's private storage on your device.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)
The app shows ads through Google AdMob, part of Google Mobile Ads. When an ad is loaded, Google may automatically receive certain information from your device, such as IP address, device model, operating system version, coarse diagnostic information, and ad-interaction data. We do not receive or store this information ourselves.

Because our game appeals to children, AdMob is configured with:

Child-directed treatment enabled (COPPA tagForChildDirectedTreatment), which disables personalized advertising and the use of the advertising ID for personalization;
Maximum ad content rating "G" (general audiences).
You can learn how Google uses this data at Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. Children's privacy
The app is designed to be appropriate for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region). Ads are served in non-personalized, child-directed mode as described above. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will help — though the app has no mechanism to transmit such information to us.

4. Device permissions the app uses
Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — optional; used only to show local reminders on your device when your pet needs care (for example, "I'm getting hungry…"). These are generated on your device; no notification data is sent anywhere. Capped at 3 per day with quiet hours from 9 pm to 8 am, and you can disable them in system settings at any time.
Internet & network state — used solely by Google AdMob to load ads. The game itself is fully playable offline.
Alarms — used to schedule the local care-reminder checks while the app is closed.
5. Data sharing and selling
We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties. The only third party involved is Google AdMob, as described in Section 2, which processes data under its own policies to serve ads.

6. Data retention and deletion
All game data is stored locally in the app's private storage. Deleting the app (or clearing its storage in Android settings) permanently removes all game data. There is nothing for us to delete on any server, because we keep nothing.

Your device may include app data in its own Android backup (Google Drive device backup) according to your device settings; that backup is controlled by you and Google, not by us.

7. Security
Because the app transmits no personal data to us, the primary protection of your information is your own device's security. Game data is stored in the app's sandboxed private storage, inaccessible to other apps on modern Android versions.

8. Changes to this policy
If we add features that change how data is handled (for example, cloud save or in-app purchases), we will update this policy and change the effective date above before those features go live.
